Functionality Producing an expanded experience

The use of tablets allows for control of content, ensuring the material presented follows the vision and ideal set forth by the museum while also allowing all patrons to enter the Enhanced Experience. The technology is intended to be expandable in the future to encompass new documents and media, cover temporary exhibitions, or reflect changes within the museum. Future iterations of the Expanded Experience will include augmented reality exhibitions.

Museum visitors can opt to participate in this expanded experience by renting a tablet with the Expanded Experience software. Visitors may then select from, focusing on different thematic routes. Depending on the preferences of the visitor, guided stops can be synced by the user to the tablet, giving them full access to the supplemental materials at the different exhibits.

These extra materials include mp4 audio files of interviews or songs as well as JPEG photos and transcriptions of primary source documents in the different sections and exhibits throughout the museum that would normally would not be available to other visitors.

A final element of the proposal is to connect the Expanded Experience with the existing #VisitorsVoices initiative already in place at the museum. The Expanded Experience will seek to encourage visitors to enter into a conversation with the museum and each other, commenting directly on exhibits or responding to the thoughts of others. Operating via the tablet allows for the museum to moderate and curate the conversation, adding the comments into the permanent digital collection.
